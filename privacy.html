Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect your name, email address, company (optional), budget range, project type, and message content. We also automatically log the page you submitted from and a timestamp for spam-prevention purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry, scope potential projects, and — if you become a client — to deliver our services. We do not sell your information to third parties or advertisers.

3. Data Storage

Contact submissions are stored in a Supabase-hosted PostgreSQL database protected by Row Level Security. Only authenticated Nexvora Studio administrators can read or manage submitted messages.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Our admin dashboard uses browser local storage to persist your authentication session. The public site does not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising pixels.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication, database, storage, and edge function hosting, and GitHub Pages for static hosting. These providers may process data on our behalf under their own privacy and security policies.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data you've submitted to us by emailing hello@nexvorastudio.com.

7. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain business records, and can be deleted on request.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@nexvorastudio.com.
